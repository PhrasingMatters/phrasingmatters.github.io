PROFESSIONAL GUITAR INSTRUCTION

Philosophy

Music is a language, and like any language, it's not just what you say but how you say it that matters. My approach to guitar instruction emphasizes the development of musical phrasing and understanding "the whole picutre" - the subtle timing, dynamics, harmony and expression that distinguish mechanical playing from heartfelt communication.
Whether you're beginning your musical journey or seeking to refine advanced techniques, we'll work together to develop your unique voice on the instrument while building a solid foundation of technical proficiency and musical understanding.
